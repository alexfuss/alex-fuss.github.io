Kurt Cobain

1967-1994

Former Frontman for Nirvana

Kurt Cobain was an American singer, songwriter, and musician, best known as the guitarist and frontman of the rock band Nirvana. Regarded as a Generation X icon, he is considered to be one of the most iconic and influential rock musicians in the history of alternative music.

The following list is a timeline for Kurt's life

  • 1967: Kurt was born at Grays Harbor Hospital in Aberdeen, Washington.
  • 1981: On his 14th birthday, Kurt was given hist first used guitar that he chose over a bike from his Uncle.
  • 1985: Kurt formed his first band called Fecal Matter after dropping out of Aberdeen High School.
  • 1989: Kurt, along with bassist Krist Novoselic and drummer Chad Channing recorded the first Nirvana record, Bleach.
  • 1991: Drummer Dave Grohl takes over from Channing and records the bands major debut record, Nevermind.
  • 1994: Kurt's body was discovered at his Lake Washington Boulevard home. He was pronounced dead on April 5, 1994 at the age of 27.
